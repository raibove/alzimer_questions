A useful project for your health

You Care. We Too.

This app will help people to identify that they have some symptoms of alzheimer. At night user have to enter details of what they ate and other special activities they performed. It will be stored in the database and questions based on it will be formed and user have to answer it on next day

Easy to use

The design is made simple so that anyone can use it at ease. It is a user-friendly website.

Detailed-Analysis

You will get a analysis on the basis of answers that you provide daily.

Privacy-friendly

The data we get from your answers is safe with us. We do not share the data with anyone.
